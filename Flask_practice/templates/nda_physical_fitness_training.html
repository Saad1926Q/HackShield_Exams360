Physical Fitness Training for NDA

Enhance your physical fitness to meet NDA requirements and excel in the physical training tests.

Exercise Type Description
Cardiovascular Exercises Activities that enhance cardiovascular endurance, such as running, cycling, and swimming.
Strength Training Exercises focused on building muscle strength, including weightlifting, bodyweight exercises, and resistance training.
Flexibility Exercises Activities to improve flexibility and range of motion, such as yoga, stretching, and Pilates.
Endurance Workouts Training sessions aimed at improving overall endurance for sustained physical activity.
Agility Drills Exercises designed to enhance agility, speed, and quick movements.
Core Strengthening Workouts focusing on strengthening the core muscles for stability and balance.
Interval Training Alternating between high-intensity and low-intensity exercises for improved fitness levels.
Obstacle Course Training Practice navigating through obstacle courses to develop agility and problem-solving skills.

Physical Training Tests

The UPSC conducts the NDA Physical Test round for the screening of the candidates who deserve a seat. This written round is followed by NDA SSB Interview, where the candidates are tested on multiple areas, including their General Awareness, Daily aptitude, overall personality, physical and soft skills, etc. Some medical tests are also included at a later stage.

Prepare for specific physical training tests conducted by the NDA. Practice regularly to improve your performance and meet the required standards.
